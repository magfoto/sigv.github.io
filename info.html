About sigv

sigv is a Max/JavaScript/Lua based live coding and composition mini-language for live performance, research and experimentation, audiovisual composition and analysis. sigv is a result of an attempt to "live code" the creation of a software instrument. This project is part of magfoto's PhD research in Digital Media and the nd::StudioLab at York University.

sigv is a live coding approach to the learning of specific functionalities of the Max environment that it was built in. This research was inspired by both the Gibberwocky project by Charlie Roberts and Graham Wakefield, and Timo Hoogland's th.gl.commandline project.
